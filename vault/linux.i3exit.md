# i3exit

> Exit the i3 window manager.
> More information: <https://gitlab.manjaro.org/packages/community/i3/i3exit>.

- Log out of i3:

`i3exit logout`

- Lock i3:

`i3exit lock`

- Shut down the system:

`i3exit shutdown`

- Suspend the system:

`i3exit suspend`

- Switch to the login screen to log in as a different user:

`i3exit switch_user`

- Hibernate the system:

`i3exit hibernate`

- Reboot the system:

`i3exit reboot`
