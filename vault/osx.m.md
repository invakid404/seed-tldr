# m

> Swiss Army Knife for macOS.

- Get the battery status:

`m battery status`

- Turn off bluetooth:

`m bluetooth off`

- List available filesystems for formatting:

`m disk filesystems`

- Enable Dock's auto hide feature:

`m dock autohide YES`

- Disable the firewall:

`m firewall disable`
