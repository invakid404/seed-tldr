# airport

> Wireless network configuration utility.

- Show current wireless status information:

`airport -I`

- Sniff wireless traffic on channel 1:

`airport sniff {{1}}`

- Scan for available wireless networks:

`airport -s`

- Disassociate from current airport network:

`sudo airport -z`
